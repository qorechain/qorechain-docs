# Quests

**Quests** are guided tasks that walk you through using QoreChain while you complete them. Each quest groups one or more steps — like funding your wallet, making a transfer, or trying a swap — and rewards you for finishing. Quests are a hands-on way to learn the network feature by feature.

Connect your wallet so your progress is tracked — see [Overview & Getting Started](/dashboard/overview#connect-your-wallet).

## Browse quests

The Quests page opens with a quick summary of how many quests are available, how many you have in progress, and how many you have completed. **Category tabs** — such as onboarding, trading, social, community, learning, daily, and weekly — let you filter the list.

Each quest appears as a card showing:

- An icon, title, and short description.
- A **difficulty** badge (for example easy, medium, or hard).
- An estimated time to complete.
- The **reward** you earn.
- A **status**: completed, in progress, started, or locked.

A locked quest lists the quest you need to finish first before it unlocks.

## Complete a quest

1. Open a quest to see its detail page, with the full list of tasks.
2. Work through each task. A task may link out to the relevant Dashboard page to perform an action, or ask you to confirm a step so it can be verified.
3. A progress bar tracks how many tasks you have completed.
4. When all tasks are done, select **Check Completion** to verify the quest and receive its reward.

Some quests reset on a daily or weekly schedule. For those, the detail page shows the reset cadence and your current streak, so you can keep a run going by returning regularly.
