# QCAIA Community Bot Overview

**QCAIA** is the official **QoreChain AI Assistant** — a community Q&A bot that answers questions about QoreChain in plain language. It is powered by **QCAI**, QoreChain's AI engine, and is currently in **beta**.

QCAIA lives where the community already is: it is available on **Telegram** and **Discord**. Ask it a question and it replies with a concise, branding-compliant answer drawn from QoreChain's official documentation.

## What QCAIA can answer

QCAIA is built to help newcomers and community members get oriented quickly. It can answer questions about:

- **Technology** — how QoreChain works at a high level, its consensus, security, and the [PRISM Consensus Engine](/architecture/prism-consensus-engine).
- **Tokenomics** — the QOR token, fee distribution, and supply.
- **Team & project** — who is behind QoreChain and where the project is headed.
- **Ecosystem** — the bridge, the AMM, rollups, the Dashboard, and other parts of the network.

If a question falls outside what QCAIA knows, it will say so rather than guess. QCAIA is a community helper, not financial or investment advice.

## Multi-language support

QCAIA understands questions in many languages. If you ask in a language other than English, it automatically translates your question, answers it, and replies — so you can use your own language and still get a clear response. See [Examples](/qcaia/examples) for a worked non-English example.

## Branding-compliance filter

Every response QCAIA produces passes through a **branding-compliance filter** before you see it. This keeps answers consistent with QoreChain's official terminology and on-brand at all times.

## Beta status

QCAIA is in **beta**. It is improving continuously, and occasionally it may not have an answer or may ask you to rephrase. Feedback from the community directly shapes how it grows.
